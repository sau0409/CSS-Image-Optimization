CSS optimization.

We use CSS media queries to provide different images for diffrent widths.

Breakpoints. We provide min and max width and all devices falling under a category will show image for that width only.

  • max-width: 800px || Image Width: 640px
  • min-width: 800px and max-width: 1200px || Image Width: 1920px
  • min-width: 1200px and max-width: 2200px || Image Width: 2400px
  • min-width: 2200px || Image Width: 6000px

We can resize the window to see the images chnaging according to break points.

Banner/Hero image with 100% width of viewport.
Content image with 50% width of viewport. For devices width greater than 600px
And for rest devices 100% width of viewport.

Apple

An apple is an edible fruit produced by an apple tree (Malus domestica). Apple trees are cultivated worldwide and are the most widely grown species in the genus Malus. The tree originated in Central Asia, where its wild ancestor, Malus sieversii, is still found today. Apples have been grown for thousands of years in Asia and Europe and were brought to North America by European colonists. Apples have religious and mythological significance in many cultures, including Norse, Greek, and European Christian tradition.
